### Record tracking examples
These cases must be covered by the record tracking mechanic
> "Sessions/Sittings" are meant as listening sessions where the user is actively listening to songs
1. Listening to one Record in one sitting form track one to the final track
2. Starting a record in one sitting, and finishing it in another session:
    1. Finishing a song in one sitting and starting the next song in line in the next sitting
    2. Stopping the session in the middle of a song and restarting that song in the next session
3. Listening to other songs in the middle of a sitting
    1. "other songs" = non relevant songs and songs in the record that are not in order
    2. From this follows that all records listened to, finished or not, should be saved in the db and that its probably unavoidable to somehow find out if the user is currently listening to an album or a playlist somehow
